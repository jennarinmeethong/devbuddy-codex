# Plan Template

## Business Summary

What business problem is being solved?

## Technical Summary

What technical changes are required?

## Affected Files

Expected files or areas to change.

## Risks

LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, or CRITICAL with reasons.

## Alternatives

Options considered and why the chosen approach is preferred.

## Open Questions

Only questions that materially affect correctness.

## Confidence Level

LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH with rationale.

## Recommendation

Wait for approval before implementation when approval is required.
